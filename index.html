Who is BTS?

BTS (Korean: 방탄소년단) is a 7 member boy group from South Korea, made up of 3 rappers (RM, SUGA, & J-HOPE) and 4 vocalists (JIN, JIMIN, V, & JUNGKOOK).
Gaining recognition from their authentic, self-produced music and top-notch performances, the group has established themselves as global superstars breaking countless world records.

Some of their top achievements include:

  • Giving their 'Speak Yourself' speech at the United Nations in 2019.
  • Raising over 2$ millions with their UNICEF LOVE MYSELF campaign.
  • Worldwide sold-out stadium tours.
  • Named as one of TIME 100: The Most Influential People of 2019.
  • Gaining three No. 1s on the Billboards Album Chart in less than a year.

What is the purpose of our project?

Our project explores BTS’ contribution to the creation of their own music in 4 areas: Vocally (singing or rapping), Writing (lyrics), Composing, and Producing.
We want to help both fans and non-fans alike to better appreciate BTS’ work and understand the authenticity of BTS’ music, by breaking down their contributions in the song-making process of their albums.

Fun facts:

  • Over 95% of the songs in our dataset are written by one or more of the BTS members.
  • Over 78% of the songs in our dataset are composed by one or more members
  • Over 21% of the songs in our dataset are produced by one or more members.

What can you do on our page?

First, chose one of the members you want to explore their contributionby clicking on one of the orbits at the top of the page!
After choosing a member to explore, a list of songs will be displayed with the following info available to view:

  • Whether the member contributed with Vocal, Writing, Composing, and Producing.
  • Album cover of each song.
  • Hovering over the album cover displays the Album name, the applicable Genre(s), and Year of Release for the specific song in the album.

You can Filter songs by:

  • Album: chose one album from the available list
  • Genre: chose one genre from the available list
  • Year of Release: chose one year from the available list
  • Contribution %: chose one of the percentages (25%, 50%, 75%, 100%). Each percentage means one type of contribution. So one percentage means that specific member only contributed in one of the 4 areas, while 100% means they contributed in all 4 parts of song creation!

You can Sort songs by:

  • Alphabetically for song names (ascending or descending)
  • Year of Release for songs (ascending or descending)
  • Contribution % (ascending or descending)

You can Search for songs and albums:

  • Just type the name of a song , and the screen will display every song with a match.
  • Or type the name of an album, and it will display all songs in that album.
  • If a song/album doesn’t display, it either means it’s not in our dataset or it is not a valid BTS song/album.
